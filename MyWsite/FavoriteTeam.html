About the Boston Celtics

The Boston Celtics are one of the most successful and storied franchises in NBA history. Founded in 1946, they are known for their passionate fanbase, legendary players, and a record of 17 NBA Championships.

Notable Players

Larry Bird
Nickname: "Larry Legend"
Known as "Larry Legend," Bird is one of the greatest players in NBA history, leading the Celtics to three championships.
Bill Russell
Nickname: "Defensive Powerhouse"
A defensive powerhouse and an 11-time NBA champion, Russell is a symbol of Celtics' dominance in the 1960s.
Paul Pierce
Nickname: "The Truth"
"The Truth" led the Celtics to their 2008 NBA Championship and was a cornerstone of the team for over a decade.
Jayson Tatum
Nickname: "The Future of the Celtics"
2024 world champion and one of this generation's best players. Tatum is known for his scoring ability and leadership.
